Domo - Appstore Publishing experience

ABOUT

This project is still under construction.

Problem

Publishing to the Appstore in its current state is extremely challenging. The process is complex and acts as a barrier for individuals looking to list their app on the platform.

Minimizing obstacles in the publishing process is crucial for the success of the Appstore.

Solution

The main objective is to remove friction to publish a listing on the new Appstore.

Keep the listing process simple, yet provide users the capabilities to publish advanced configurations for complex listings.

Allow users to start creating and editing a listing separately from adding assets.
